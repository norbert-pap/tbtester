Define Your Company Purpose and Mission

1. Our purpose is to [verb/goal] [target audience/world/industry] by [how/what you do].
Example: Our purpose is to empower small businesses by providing easy-to-use financial tools.
2. We exist to [verb/goal] so that [desired impact/outcome].
Example: We exist to create affordable healthcare solutions so that everyone can access quality care.
3. We’re on a mission to [verb/goal] for [audience/beneficiary], [how/why].
Example: We’re on a mission to simplify travel for families, making every trip stress-free and memorable.
